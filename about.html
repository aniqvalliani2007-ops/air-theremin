About the Project

Air Theremin is an experimental web-based musical instrument that translates your hand gestures into sound. It pays homage to the original Theremin—the world's first electronic instrument that is played without physical contact.

Powered by MediaPipe's AI hand tracking and the Web Audio API, the application detects the precise position of your index finger in real time through your webcam. By hovering your finger over a virtual note ring, you trigger different frequencies with zero latency.

The project aims to showcase the incredible possibilities of browser-based AI and interactive audio synthesis, packaged in a sleek, minimalistic, and highly responsive interface. No apps to install, no hardware required—just you and your browser.
